06Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Scope: Problem Space Only

SIGNAL AI

Status: APPROVED

Executive Summary

Founders spend more time researching who to contact than actually selling — and when they do reach out, it is at the wrong moment. Signal AI exists to fix both problems at once.

Founder-led GTM is the highest-leverage sales motion in B2B. A founder's brand, credibility, and authentic voice converts at 3–5× the rate of any hired rep. Yet founders are losing this advantage to an invisible tax: hours of manual research, fragmented tools, and zero signal infrastructure to tell them when a prospect is actually ready to buy.

The result is painfully predictable. Cold outreach reply rates have collapsed to 3.4% industry-wide. Founders spend only 30% of their time selling. And the signals that would tell them the perfect moment to reach out — a promotion, a funding round, a job change, a LinkedIn post — are buried across dozens of platforms they cannot monitor simultaneously.

Problem Statement

For founders doing their own GTM, the status quo creates three compounding failures:

  • The Research Tax: Founders spend 40–70% of their potential selling time doing manual research. This is intelligence gathering that a well-designed system could automate.
  • The Timing Blind Spot: A prospect who just received a promotion is 14× more likely to respond. 99% of companies fail to respond within 5 minutes of a buying signal firing. Responding 5 minutes faster yields 9× higher conversion.
  • The Personalization Illusion: Generic AI tools can reference a prospect's job title, but cannot tell the founder why this specific person is likely to care right now.

Market Opportunity

The sales intelligence market reached $33.1 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach $90.2 billion by 2034. The founder-led GTM segment represents approximately 220,000 companies in North America and Europe alone. At a $99–499/month price point, the SAM is approximately $12–26 billion annually. This is a market that currently has no purpose-built tool serving it.

The tools that founders can actually afford ($49–185/month) offer zero signal intelligence — just contact databases and email sending. There is a massive white space between 'what founders can afford' and 'what actually works'.

Why Now

  • Cold Outreach is Structurally Broken: Reply rates dropped from 85% in 2019 to 3.4% in 2026.
  • GTM Stack is Consolidating: Buyers want fewer, deeper platforms over point solutions.
  • AI Agents: Real-time signal monitoring is now economically viable for SMB price points.
  • Founder-Led GTM Growth: Founders are carrying the GTM burden longer than ever.

08Retrospective & Learnings

Rate-Limiting Risks

Heavy dependence on LinkedIn signals poses significant rate-limiting risks. The architecture must diversify across 15+ sources (e.g., Crunchbase, press releases) from launch to avoid catastrophic data pipeline failure.

Context vs Data

Founders don't just need to know *what* happened; they need to know *why* it matters. The jump from signal detection to AI-drafted context is where the real value is unlocked, shifting the product from an alert system to an execution engine.
