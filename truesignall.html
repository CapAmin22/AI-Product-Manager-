01Executive Summary

The One-Line Thesis

Founders spend more time researching who to contact than actually selling — and when they do reach out, it is at the wrong moment. Signal AI exists to fix both problems at once.

Founder-led GTM is the highest-leverage sales motion in B2B. A founder's brand, credibility, and authentic voice converts at 3–5× the rate of any hired rep. Yet founders are losing this advantage to an invisible tax: hours of manual research, fragmented tools, and zero signal infrastructure to tell them when a prospect is actually ready to buy.

The result is painfully predictable. Cold outreach reply rates have collapsed to 3.4% industry-wide. Founders spend only 30% of their time selling. And the signals that would tell them the perfect moment to reach out are buried across dozens of platforms they cannot monitor simultaneously.

02Problem Statement

The B2B outbound market is in structural crisis. Buyers have deployed AI-powered spam filters. Sellers have responded with AI volume. The result is a race to the bottom. For founders doing their own GTM, this creates three compounding failures:

search

The Research Tax

Founders spend 40–70% of their potential selling time doing manual research: LinkedIn stalking, Crunchbase scanning, Google alerts. This is intelligence gathering that a well-designed system could automate.

Validated Pain

Founders are stitching 17 tools together manually, losing 10–15 hours per week to data normalization.

schedule

The Timing Blind Spot

A prospect who just received a promotion is 14× more likely to respond. A company that just raised a Series B will finalize vendors within 90 days. Founders miss these windows because they have no monitoring infrastructure.

Core Timing Problem

99% of companies fail to respond within 5 mins of a buying signal. Being 5 mins faster yields 9× higher conversion.

psychology

The Personalization Illusion

Generic AI tools can reference a job title, but cannot tell the founder why this specific person is likely to care right now. Without signal context, even well-written outreach reads as noise.

The Context Collapse

When a founder books a meeting, they lack the specific recent news or internal politics the prospect is prioritizing.

03Target User & Personas

Primary Persona — The Founder-Seller

A B2B SaaS or professional services founder at Seed to Series B stage ($0–15M ARR) who is personally responsible for GTM. They are time-constrained: they carry the full weight of product, team, and sales simultaneously.

Company Stage Seed → Series B
Team Size 1–50 employees
Deal Size $5K–$500K ACV
Budget $100–$500/mo

Core Job-To-Be-Done: "Tell me the right person, the right moment, and the right thing to say — then get out of my way."

Secondary Persona

The Early GTM Hire: The first sales or growth hire at a founder-led company. They need the same signal intelligence but lack the founder's network authority, relying heavily on structured signals and AI-drafted context.

Anti-ICP (Who this is NOT for)

High-volume outbound agencies, enterprise SDR teams, or any team depending on sending 10,000+ emails per week. This is not a blast tool.

05Jobs To Be Done

1

When managing 80+ target accounts, I want to be notified the moment a genuine connection opportunity arises so I can reach out first and authentically.

Current Gap: No tool does real-time signal monitoring at founder price.

2

When preparing outreach, I want the AI to tell me exactly why this person is worth reaching out to right now, so I can write a message that feels personal.

Current Gap: Signal and outreach tools are separate products.

3

When I prepare for a first call, I want a 2-minute brief that tells me everything relevant about this person and company right now.

Current Gap: Relies on manual research or expensive Copilot tools.
